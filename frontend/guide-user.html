Student Guide

Learn how to interpret your dashboard stats, ML risk predictions, and AI advisor.

1

Check Your Classifications

Your dashboard assigns a Classification badge (Good / Average / At-Risk) based strictly on your historical exams. The Machine Learning Risk badge (Low / Medium / High) predicts future struggles using a combination of attendance, assignments, and exam grades.

2

Explore Visualizations

The dashboard features percentages clearly labeled on your Distribution Pie Charts so you easily understand how your effort is split across metrics. Keep an eye on the Performance Trend Line to ensure your scores are rising.

3

AI Academic Advisor Enhancements

Click the 🤖 chatbot button on the bottom right. Type `roadmap`. If you are struggling, the bot will craft an intense 4-week recovery course. If you are already at the top of your class, the bot will suggest Advanced Enhancement Tips like entering hackathons or studying research publications.
