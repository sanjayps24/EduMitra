Admin Guide

Learn how to manage students, interpret visual analytics, and address alerts.

1

Create & Manage Students

Click the "➕ Add Student" button on the dashboard to register a new student. Click the ✏️ edit button on any student row to update their details or add exam records.

2

Visual Analytics

The dashboard features interactive charts. The Pie Chart automatically categorizes students into Performance brackets with exact percentages. The Bar Chart compares Average Exams vs Attendance across departments.

3

Automated Alerts

If any student drops below a 50% attendance rate or scores a sub-40% exam average, a red High-Risk Alert Banner will automatically appear at the top of the dashboard. Address these immediately.

4

Real-time Badges

Modifying a student's academic record will instantly trigger the Machine Learning pipeline. The student's "Risk Classification" badges will reload and reflect their new status instantly.

5

View Student Profile

Click any student row to drill down into their personal radar charts, performance trends, and risk analysis metrics.
